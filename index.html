(01) About me

Hello, I'm Chaahat, strategist, storyteller, and chronic over-thinker of captions.

I'm a content strategist and social media marketer who believes the best brands don't shout louder, they say something worth hearing. I build the systems behind that: voice, narrative, calendar, and channel, turning scattered posting into a strategy audiences actually feel.

From audience research and campaign planning to creative execution, my job is connecting what a brand wants to say with what its people want to hear. The result is content with purpose, not just presence.
